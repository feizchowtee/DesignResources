DesignResources

A curated list of tools & resources to help designers and developers make beautiful things.

Typography

Typography

Google Fonts

A directory of fonts to use for your projects. Free for personal and commercial use.

Lost Type

A pay-what-you-want type foundry.

TypeWolf

Typewolf helps designers choose the perfect font combination for their next design project

Fontface Ninja

Fontface Ninja is a free browser extension that let you inspect, try, buy and bookmark any fonts on any website.

Font Squirrel

Huge collection of free fonts will commercial licenses. Also has a webfont generator that's super useful.

TLOMT

The League Of Moveable Type. A completely open-source of high quality fonts to use however you please.

Font Joy

Another awesome font pairing tool.

Better Web Type

An amazing guide to web typography. By Matej Latin

Font Shop

Huge List of free and almost free fonts to use in your projects.

Dafont

Huge archive of free to download fonts.

Font Flame

Like Tinder but for fonts.

Adobe Fonts

Premium Fonts that come with your Adobe CC subscription.

Color Inspiration

Color
Inspiration

Picular

Like Google but for colors.

Color Hunt

Color Hunt is an free and open source platform for color inspiration with thousands of hand picked palettes.

Coolors

Generate color palettes and save them.

Adobe Kuler

A fun color palette generator by Adobe.

Web Gradients

WebGradients is a free collection of 180 linear gradients that you can use as content backdrops in any part of your website.

Color Box by Lyft

Colorbox is a tool to produce color sets open-sourced by Lyft's design team

Palettable

Start off with one color and build your color palette from there.

Color Wise

Color Wise generates color palettes from the most voted products on Product Hunt.

Color Drop

Browse and save trendy color palettes.

Iconography

Icono-graphy

The Noun Project

A massive collection of over a million icons. You can attribute the author on use or pay for a membership.

Font Awesome

Hundreds of icons to use in web and/or design projects. For web, just include the CDN and you're good to go.

Icon Monstr

Like FontAwesome, Icon Monstr have a nice collection of icons to use for web and graphic design. They also provide a CDN link to include in your web projects for fast loading.

Ikonate

Huge Collection of easily customizable and accessible vector icons.

Illustrio

A 100% free and customizable icon library.

Feather

A minimalist open-source icon library.

Ionicons

Free high quality icon library with svg and webfont support.

Stock Photography

Stock-Photography

Unsplash

Collection of very high quality and high resolution photos from photographers all over the world. While user attribution to the author is not required it's still encouraged.

Pexels

An alternative to Unsplash. Very high quality photos from talented photographers around the globe.

Death to the Stock Photo

Subscription based platform. They have a 14-day trial and two membership options. You can also join their mailing list to occasional photo packs for free.

The Stocks

Get free photos, mockups, icons , videos and more from sources all over the internet.

Illustrations & Graphics

Illustrations

UnDraw

A growing collection of beautiful illustrations available to download for free in PNG or SVG format. Also features an on the fly color generator for vectors before download.

Lukasz Adam

Various vector art and icons free for commercial and personal use made by independent web designer Lukasz Adam

Humaaans

Humaaans is a mix and match illustration library of humans free to use in your designs.

DrawKit

A free SVG illustration library available to use in your projects.
